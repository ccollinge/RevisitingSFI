# RevisitingSFI
Reproducible code for analyses used to "revisit" the social frailty index in aging adults as presented in:

Collinge, CW, Bartolomucci, A, & Luciana M. (2026). Revisiting the social frailty index in aging adults: Relevance and implications for an emerging construct. Geroscience. https://doi.org/10.1007/s11357-026-02183-1

The paper includes analyses from 2 large longitudinal studies on aging adults in the US: the Health and Retirement Study (HRS) and the Midlife in the United States (MIDUS) study. The files here are separated by study.

Data from the HRS used in this study is available through registration at: https://hrs.isr.umich.edu/. Data from MIDUS used in this study is available through registration at: https://midus.colectica.org/.
